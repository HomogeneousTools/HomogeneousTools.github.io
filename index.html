HomogeneousTools

HomogeneousTools is a toolset to work with homogeneous varieties and homogeneous vector bundles on them, mostly with a view towards sheaf cohomology.

It is still a work-in-progress, developed by

  • Pieter Belmans
  • Javier Fernandez Piriz

and with contributions by

  • Max Briest
  • Maxim Smirnov

If you want a sneak preview, please get in touch with Pieter
